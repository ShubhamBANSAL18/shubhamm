Vice-Chancellor

(YMCAUST,Faridabad)
Mr.Dinesh Kumar
Prof. Dinesh Kumar joined YMCA University of Science & Technology, Faridabad as the Vice-Chancellor on 4.11.2015. Recently, Prof. Kumar has been awarded the prestigious Indian Science Congress Association Homi J Bhabha Gold Medal by Prime Minister of India. Previously he has worked as Director of UIET from August 2010 to January 2015. He has been Chairman, Department of Electronic Science at Kurukshetra University from 1.05.2006- 30.04.2009 and then again from 1.05.2013-3.11.2015. He has worked as Director, Kurukshetra University Alumni Association for many years and generated funds to the tune of Rs 5 Crores.
